About Me

Welcome to my website! I am currently an undergraduate student at the University of Wisconsin - Madison pursuing a major in Journalism with a certificate in Digital Media Studies and Business. I am undecided in concentrations between Broadcast and Reporting and Strategic Communication. Nevertheless, I am interested in exploring a wide span of issues in news media. Topics I find particularly engaging are protest coverage, international relations, women's issues, and cultural patterns in the entertainment industry

Growing up in New York City, I was privileged enough to be exposed to the hustle and bustle of a large cosmopolitan city. Attending a highschool located in Times Square I was in the epicenter of working professionals, Broadway stars, statistics about crime and homelessness, political TED talks, and international conferences daily. Something different was occuring on every avenue and the news organizations headquarters were all at the frontlines of the action.

Now residing in Madison, I miss the pace of Manhattan and how news permeates the city blocks.
